Author Guidelines

Before You Submit

  • Confirm your manuscript fits the Aims & Scope.
  • One corresponding author should register and submit on behalf of all authors.
  • Ensure all authors approve the final version and submission.

Manuscript Preparation

  • Format: PDF, max 10 MB (system enforces this). Use clear fonts and numbered sections.
  • Structure (recommended):
    1. Title, Authors (with affiliations), Abstract (150–250 words), Keywords (3–6)
    2. Introduction & Related Work
    3. Methods / Theory
    4. Results / Experiments
    5. Discussion / Limitations
    6. Conclusion & Future Work
    7. References
    8. Appendix / Supplementary (optional)
  • References: Any consistent scholarly style is acceptable (e.g., IEEE, APA, AMS).
  • Anonymity: Reviews may be single-blind by default (reviewer identity hidden from authors).
  • Ethics: Disclose conflicts of interest, sensitive data, and any human/animal study approvals.
  • Reproducibility (when applicable): Provide datasets/code URLs in the text or as supplements.

How to Submit

  1. Register (or log in).
  2. Open New Submission and fill in all required fields.
  3. Attach your manuscript PDF and submit. You can track status from your Dashboard.

After Submission

  • Initial Check: Editorial screening for scope/format.
  • Peer Review: Assigned to a reviewer; decisions include Accept / Minor / Major Revision / Reject.
  • Revisions: You may be asked to revise and re-upload your PDF.

Questions? See Policies or Contact.
