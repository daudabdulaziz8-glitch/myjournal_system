Journal Policies

Publication Ethics

  • Submissions must be original and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • All authors should have contributed substantially and approve submission.
  • Conflicts of interest must be disclosed in the manuscript.
  • Where applicable, provide approvals for studies involving human/animal subjects.

Peer Review

  • Single-blind: reviewer identity is hidden from authors.
  • Decisions: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject.
  • Revised submissions should clearly highlight changes and responses to reviews.

Open Access

FACOMS Journal provides open access to accepted articles to support wider dissemination of research in Computing and Mathematical Sciences.

Copyright & Licensing

Authors retain copyright of their work. By default, we encourage a permissive license (e.g., CC BY 4.0) where appropriate. Department or university policies may apply.

Data & Code Availability

Authors are encouraged to share datasets, code, and models whenever feasible, with clear documentation and citation instructions.

Plagiarism Screening

Submissions may be screened using plagiarism detection tools; suspected cases will be investigated.
